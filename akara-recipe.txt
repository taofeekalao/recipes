Ingridients
	1. Groundnut Oil
	2. Beans
	3. Fresh Water
	4. Cooking Salt
	5. Fresh Pepper


Dirctions
	1.  Soak Beans In Water For Sometime
	2.	Remove The Outer Cover
	3.	Rinse Beans And Ground Into Powder
	4.	Add Pepper To Beans
	5.	Put Oil On Fire
	6.	Add Little Mixture Of Beans And Pepper To Oil
	7.	Fry For Some Minutes Until Mixture Turns Brownish
	8.	Remove From Oil
